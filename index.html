About Me

Hello! I'm Sayantan, a cybersecurity professional with a passion for offensive security and threat mitigation. My technical expertise includes manual penetration testing of web applications and APIs, cloud security assessments, and implementing secure SDLC practices.

With a strong background in Vulnerability Assessment and Penetration Testing (VAPT), Red Teaming, and Cloud Security, I specialize in identifying and remediating security flaws. I thrive on the challenge of simulating real-world attacks to strengthen defenses and ensure robust security postures.

Beyond my professional role, I am co-founder of BSides Kolkata, part of many security and technical communities and I enjoy mentoring aspiring cybersecurity professionals.
